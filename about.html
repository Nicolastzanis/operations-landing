Our story

Operations Tool was born from direct experience inside the forex and CFD brokerage world. We watched operations teams waste hours every day switching between MT5 Manager windows, exporting data to spreadsheets, and manually checking risk positions one account at a time.

The tools that existed were either too complex, too expensive, or simply not built for how real operations teams work. So we built our own — focused entirely on the six things an MT5 broker ops team needs to see every single day.

The result is a platform that gets out of your way and lets your team focus on what matters: keeping the brokerage running smoothly.

Our principles

Real-time first

Data that is 30 seconds old is useless in brokerage operations. Every number on the platform is live, updated via WebSocket, with no manual refresh.

Built for the job

We only build what operations teams actually use. No bloat, no features added for the sake of a slide deck. Every module solves a real daily problem.

Simple to adopt

No training sessions, no complex onboarding. If your team has used MT5 Manager, they will understand this platform within minutes of logging in.
