Everything you need

One platform for your entire ops team

Every tool your MT5 operations team needs, connected in a single secure dashboard.

Pricing Monitor

Live bid/ask prices and spreads for all your symbols in one place. Instantly spot anomalies, widen spreads, or symbols going off-market.

Risk Monitor

Real-time account equity, margin level, and floating P&L across all connected accounts. Color-coded alerts when margin gets critical.

Client Lookup

Instantly search clients by login, name, or email. View their open positions, account history, and group settings without logging into MT5.

Swaps & Group Simulator

View current swap rates across all groups and symbols. Simulate changes before applying them — see the impact before your clients do.

Email & Telegram Alerts

Get notified the moment something needs attention — margin calls, spread spikes, server disconnects. Delivered to email and Telegram instantly.

Symbol Hours & Holidays

Manage trading hours and market holiday schedules for all symbols. Never accidentally leave markets open during exchange closures.
