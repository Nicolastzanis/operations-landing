What We Do

Six modules, one platform. Every tool your MT5 operations team needs, connected in a single secure dashboard.

Pricing Monitor

Live bid/ask prices and spreads for all your symbols. Spot anomalies and off-market prices the moment they happen.

Risk Monitor

Real-time equity, margin level, and floating P&L. Color-coded alerts automatically flag accounts approaching margin call.

Client Lookup

Search any client instantly by login, email, or name. See their open positions and account settings without touching MT5.

Swaps & Group Simulator

View current swap rates for every group and symbol. Simulate changes and preview the impact before your clients see it.

Email & Telegram Alerts

Instant notifications for margin calls, spread spikes, and server events. Delivered to your inbox and Telegram the second they trigger.

Symbol Hours & Holidays

Manage trading hours and market holiday schedules for every symbol. Never leave a market open during an exchange closure again.
