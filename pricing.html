Frequently asked questions

How do I pay?
We'll walk you through pricing and accepted payment methods once we've discussed your brokerage's needs. Get in touch to get started.
Is there a setup fee or contract?
No setup fee, no minimum contract. You pay monthly and can cancel at any time. If you cancel, your access remains until the end of the paid month.
How many team members can I add?
Unlimited. There is no per-seat pricing. You can add your entire operations team, compliance team, support staff and management under the same subscription.
Do I need to install anything on my MT5 server?
No installation required on your server. We connect via your existing MT5 Manager API using manager credentials you provide. You keep full control and can revoke access at any time.
Can I try it before paying?
Contact us and we can arrange a live demo on a demo MT5 account so you can see the platform with real data before committing.
What happens after I get in touch?
We'll discuss your brokerage's needs and put together a quote. Once you're ready to proceed, we'll send you a registration link and payment instructions. After payment is confirmed, your account is activated and you can connect your MT5 server and invite your team.
