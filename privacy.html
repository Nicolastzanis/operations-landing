Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Operations Tool ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform and website.

1. Information we collect

Account information

When you register, we collect your company name, email address, and a password (stored as a secure hash). We also store your company's MT5 server connection credentials, which are used solely to connect to your MT5 Manager API.

Usage data

We may collect information about how the platform is used, including pages visited, features accessed, and actions taken. This helps us improve the product.

Payment information

We do not store credit card numbers or bank details. Payments are made via cryptocurrency. We record the transaction hash you submit for payment verification purposes only.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain the platform
  • To authenticate your account and control access
  • To connect to your MT5 server on your behalf
  • To send important service notifications and alerts
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns

3. MT5 server data

The platform connects to your MT5 server using credentials you provide. Data retrieved from your MT5 server (account data, pricing, positions) is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers beyond what is necessary to display it to your authorised users.

Your MT5 Manager credentials are encrypted at rest and are never shared with third parties.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We may share data with service providers who assist in operating the platform (hosting, email delivery), under strict confidentiality obligations.

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Operations Tool or our users.

5. Data retention

We retain account data for as long as your subscription is active. If you cancel, your data is retained for 30 days to allow reactivation, then deleted. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, password hashing, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies

The platform uses session cookies for authentication only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. The marketing website may use basic analytics to understand visitor numbers.

8. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@operationstool.io.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@operationstool.io.
